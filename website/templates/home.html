WHO ARE WE

Heavily Iconic Investments is a 100% black empowered company. The primary focus of Heavily Iconic Investments is in the distribution of petroleum products, diesels, gas, unleaded 95 and paraffin.

Heavily Iconic Investments was established in 2019, with vest experience of distributing petroleum products, the directors saw a need to start Heavily Iconic Investments to expand the business further into a wholesale company. Heavily Iconic Investments is BEE Level 1.

We are committed to the development and expansion of the fuel distribution industry in South Africa, particularly where this will benefit smaller players.

To achieve this, we are committed making investments developing storage and transport infrastructure because the existing infrastructure is generally not accessible to the emerging players. In this regard, we are very keen to enter into collaborative arrangements with independent players in the arena in order to meet our respective objectives.

Mission

  • To house and provide / distribute the best petroleum products with energy to strive for improvement in and around South Africa.

Vision

  • To have Heavily Iconic Investments as a leader among petroleum wholesale companies in the South African region demonstrating a high level of social and ecological responsibility and providing its clients with up-to-date services.

Future Growth

  • It leans on the vision of Nelson Mandela “Our long-term structural development depends on all of Southern Africa achieving ‘positive growth’ and on building economic ties and development co-operation amongst ourselves.”
